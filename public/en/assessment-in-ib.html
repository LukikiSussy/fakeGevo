Assessment in IB

IB grades are given on a scale of 1-7, with 7 being the best grade. After each semester, the student receives a grade in each subject converted to a Czech scale of 1 - 5. The final grade in each subject (equivalent to a high school diploma) consists of a section that is assessed directly at school and on which the student works long-term and tests that are assessed externally.
